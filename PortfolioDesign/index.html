My name is Victoria (Torie) Szczech adn I am 20 years old. I'm a second semester sophomore at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire, and I am majoring in Psychology and minoring in Multimedia Communication. I love to spend time with my boyfriend, niece and nephews, and loved ones.

I have always had a love for capturing memories through a lense (my camera roll can account for that). I started talking pictures when I was in elementary school and since then it seems like I can't put the camera down. In my senior year of high school I was Editor-in-Chief of the Yearbook Staff, which meant I was in charge of overseeing everything from pictures, to editing, to finalizing every page. I graduated at the top of my class while being Editor-in-Chief for Yearbook, president of Student Council, Vice President of NHS, and was a member of FFA and FBLA. More recently, after having a very hard Sophomore year in college, I decided that I wanted to really put my heart into what I truly loved: photography. So, I put down my moms camera that I had been using for years and decided to make a big girl purchase and buy my own camera. From there I started my journey as VSCheck Photography.

Right now, in addition to starting up my photography business, I work part time at a boutique in my hometown, Broadway Boutique, where I manage the social media, and I help customers find clothing they feel beautiful in.

I'll step you through the name behind "VSCheck Photography." The "VS" are my initials, "Victoria Szczech." The "Check" is how you say my last name 'Szczech.' Yep, just like a check mark; it's very decieving. Which is why my logo is shaped like a check mark. Then "Photography" is just my interest so that's why that is in my name.

Thank you for taking time out of your day to look at my portfolio and I hope you enjoy all the photos as much as I do!!
